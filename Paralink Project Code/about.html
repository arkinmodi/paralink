EVERYONE HAS A STORY. HERE'S OURS.



ParaLink is a networking site designed specifically for the disabled community. The goal of the site is to allow registered members to establish networks with people they trust professionally. It connects the world's professionals to make them more productive and successful. When you join ParaLink, you get access to people, jobs, and insights that help you be great at whatever you do.

Our Story

We are four IBT students from North Park S.S. who have had an amazing journey coming to where we are today. After working on ParaLink, teamwork has become our strength. We built this app with a noble motive to bring disabled people together. A perfect blend of creative and technical students have made ParaLink!
